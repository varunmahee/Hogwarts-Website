How To Find Us

Students, the only way to get to the Hogwarts Express is by arriving at platform 9 3/4. You can arrive at that platform by directly going through the platforms 9 and 10.

Do not fear, you will not crash into the wall, but instead will be magically transported to platform 9 3/4. You will then board the Hogwarts Express (which leaves at 11 a.m.) and will soon reach Hogwarts castle.

The school year starts on September 1 and ends in the last week of June. We hope to see you this year.

Sincerely,

Headmaster Dumbledore
